Salmon Grain Bowl

Calories per serving: 592         Diets: Gluten free & High protien
Yield: 2

Ingredients

Veggie Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup chopped broccoli
  • 8 oz. shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp liquid aminos
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup (or coconut nectar)
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1-2 Tbsp of water

Bowl Ingredients:

  • 2-4 oz. pieces of salmon
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 Tbsp basil pesto
  • lemon wedge
  • sesame oil
  • handful of sprouts
  • handful of lettuce
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper and spread potatoes in an even layer. Cook for 15 minutes, stirring once.
  2. In a pan, combine the remaining sweet potato ingredients (miso, liquid aminos, lemon juice, garlic, sesame seeds, coconut nectar, and water). Bring this to a simmer and let thicken. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. On a separate baking sheet with foil season salmon with some sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Once the potatoes have cooked for 15 minutes, put the salmon in the oven and add the mushrooms and broccoli to the potatoes, cook for 12 minutes or until salmon is cooked to your liking.
  5. While salmon and potatoes continue cooking, cook your quinoa.
  6. When veggies are a little chared from the oven saute them in the veggie sauce until they are well coated.
  7. It is time to plate! Start with grains at the bottom, then lettuce, salmon, veggies, and sprouts. Garnish with a spoonful of basil pesto.
