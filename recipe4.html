Sweet Potato Brownie

Calories per serving: 168         Diets: Gluten free & Vegan
Yield: 12

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mashed sweet potato (steamed then mashed)
  • 1/2 cup creamy almond butter
  • 1/2 cacao powder or unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/3 cup gluten-free flour mix
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp himalayan salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan dark chocolate chips (optional)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9-inch square pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed potato, almond butter, cacao powder, coconut sugar, flour, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir well, until batter is smooth.
  3. If using chocolate chips, fold into batter.
  4. Transfer the batter to the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake at 350°F until the edges look dry and the center of the brownies have puffed up, about 35 to 40 minutes.
  5. Let brownies cool before slicing into pieces.

Notes:
Brownies should be stored in an airtight container in the fridge. Extras may also be stored in the freezer. Leftovers can be stored in the fridge for up to a week, or in the freezer for up to 3 months.
